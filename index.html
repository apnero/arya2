Ariel Classes

Monday

Pole is a journey of ones strength and perseverance. In this class you feel the excitement of inverting and ambition to develop personal pole goals. Remember bruises are badges of honor and a step closer to achieving your goals!

Pole Control is designed to incorporate the strength and fluidity learned in Pole Flow and Pole High to comfortably begin the intermediate levels of inverting, variations on climbing, shoulder mounts and more intricate spins/tricks. This class gives the student the foundation of body control, confidence with the concept of inverting, and builds the center core strength necessary. Different variations and adjuncts of climb will in introduced and developed based upon skill level.

Basic handstands/dismounts from inversions will be explored as well as spins that may require more technique and strength. Spin pole will be used.

This class is not for the faint at heart, definitely a huge challenge with great rewards on a student pole progression.

This class is designed for the hardcore pole students that are interested in building their pole stamina and strength. Pole Powerhouse is constructed from a bootcamp perspective of pole. It is required that the student is completely comfortable inverting on their own. Training will include consecutive inversions and pole climbs. Students will benefit greatly from the strength and consistency that will be developed in this class.

Spinner Pole will be used.

The beginning stage of pole fitness. Your feet are still on the ground or hovering. You will learn the mechanics: different grips, walking around the pole, proximity to the pole, weight distribution, momentum and speed control. Our goal is to get you comfortable around the pole while conditioning your muscles to be able to advance. You will learn the basic spins and climbing. Pole Flow is a minimum of 8 classes series, after which members will be assessed by the instructor and either advance to the next level (Pole High - Level 2) or continue in the current level. We want members to progress when they are ready.

* This is a continuously run 8-week series of classes.

If you have never taken an aerial hoop (Lyra) class and are new to aerial apparatus, this is the class for you. In this class you will learn the various techniques to enter and exit from the hoop along with conditioning, strength and flexibility exercises.

Aerial hoop (aka lyra) is a circular apparatus suspended from a single point. Build strength, showcase flexibility and learn to flip, turn, roll, pose, and transition between positions. Classes begin with basic skills and conditioning and gradually progress into fluid choreographed sequences.

In this class you will use aerial hammocks to achieve weightlessness, spinal decompression, gentle stretching, and core stabilization. Allow the sensation of flight to bring playfulness and freedom to your fitness class. Decompress and elongate your spine through weightless inversions. The hammock supports your body’s contours, allowing you to go deeper into stretches and traditional yoga poses. Finally, let the gentle swinging of the hammock relax your mind and release your daily stress.

Hammock, also called Sling, is one piece of fabric with the two tails connected to the ceiling at the same point to create a trapeze-like apparatus. In this class you will learn drops, slides, and add spinning.

Tuesday

Moving up in the pole world! This is the next step in Arya's Pole Fitness Classes. In this class you will perfect a variety of climbs and dismounts from the pole. You will also learn the beginnings of inversions and gain different grips with your hands and thighs. Students will learn how to properly invert and dismount from an upside down position. Basic inversion tricks will also be taught and mastered before progressing to the next level.

This class works on becoming comfortable up high on the pole as well as being upside down.

Pole-finity and BEYOND! Infinite numbers of tricks and transitions, new tricks developing everyday!

Wednesday

Polesque is a routine based class designated to one specific song which includes a video produced at the end of the session. This class is done in either heels or bare feet depending on the dynamic of the song selected. The routine includes a handspring combination, dance sequence, floor work, and spin/static pole combination. Routines are usually within 3-4minutes. The hard work and development of the routine is a rewarding experience for the student. A new routine is produced every 8 weeks.

Advanced Hoop classes are for those students who have demonstrated proficiency with our level 1 class on the apparatus. These classes focus on more complex skills, fluid transitions, and short sequences. You’ll spend more time up in the air building endurance and stamina, as well as strength. If interested, Level 2 students with instructor permission may begin building their own choreography and join our choreography classes and student showcases.

Thursday

The name speaks for itself! Pole students who make it this far are definitely addicted! There is no going back now!

Pole addict is designed for the students that have mastered the basics of inversions; ready to explore combinations, transitions, and intermediate inversions. With this class the student will expand their catalogue of inversions and begin understanding the concepts of transitioning from one trick another smoothly. It is required the student is comfortable with basic inversions and ready to take it to the next level of inverted combinations. Spin pole will be used.

Grab those sexy heels and get ready to be captivated by the power of sensual dance. You will quickly become addicted to this class. You will build strength, body awareness and confidence in this alluring work out. You will learn sensual and sometimes naughty pole dance routines and trick combinations. The tricks will focus on flow and dance movements on and around the pole as well as the floor . In addition to learning tricks we will learn transitions to connect them together to perform a seamless routine and heel clacks that will entice and captivate any audience. Warning this class will leave you feeling confident and inspired to come back for more. Ladies please bring heels for full experience!
