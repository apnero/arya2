Arya Fitness

Arya Fitness Studio is a women’s only studio that emphasizes the idea of having fun while getting fit! Arya’s unique selection of classes allows each individual a chance to better their lives and achieve all their fitness goals.

Whether if its hanging upside-down and wrapping your body in a silk hammock, bending and twisting your body gracefully around a circle in the sky during aerial hoop, learning intricate and mesmerizing moves on a chrome pole, or taking one of our fun and challenging fitness classes…we have a place for everyone in the studio.

Arya Fitness Studio promotes not only the building of muscles, but also self confidence, self love and friendships. This studio is designed to bring women together in an atmosphere of positivity, where each member shares in the desire to become healthier in body, mind and soul….

We are the only female focused fitness studio in Fairfield County offering a variety of aerial arts classes! Fly your way to fitness with our Aerial Arts classes!

Small Classes...Intimate Setting...Empowering and Elevating Your Soul!

Daniela Nero

  • Owner, Fitness Instructor
  • NAFC Personal Training Certified
  • NAFC Nutrition Coach Certified
  • Spinning and PiYo Certifiend

Growing up in Brazil, a professional ballerina for 15 years…Daniela has always loved music and movement. Having 2 daughters, Daniela has worked hard to get back in shape, and fitness was an essential tool to get her body back in shape. Daniela has always dreamt of owning a fitness studio of her own, and when the opportunity to start Arya Fitness Studio came along, she was ready for the journey!

She fell in love with rode cycling and swimming and this past summer she completed 2 triathlons and her first century ride. She’s a certified spinning instructor and recently completed her personal training and nutrition certification. She’s passionate about creating a community of women who feel empowered and beautiful!

Daniela brings high energy, motivation, inspiration and a challenge to her classes that will leave you wanting more! She currently teaches a variety of fitness classes and basic aerial hammock.

Christine Anderson

Christine is an extremely passionate and dedicated Pole fitness Instructor. She began her pole journey in 2011. Pole dancing quickly became a passion and obsession. Her drive to reach the next level as an athlete and artist has led her to train with Pole Fitness Champions and Elite Industry professionals. She also competed in 2013 at the SuperShag Pole Fitness Championships in Boston, Ma.

Christine embraces the sensual side of pole dance. Her specialties are flow and movement, floor work and flexibility. She is inspired everyday by the empowerment that pole fitness gives to women. She believes all women can gain confidence and self acceptance through body awareness. Instructing Pole fitness brings great happiness into her world. Christine feels honored and thankful to be instructing such a strong, supportive and inspirational group of women.
